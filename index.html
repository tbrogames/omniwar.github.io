SS-17

Jumper’s home, SS-17, is a space station orbiting twin planets and asteroid fields.
It is composed of a large main corridor, raised platforms, several tunnel systems, sliding doors, lasers, exploding barrels, and a lift.

Subterra

Khalli's home, Subterra, is a vast underground city composed of multiple castles and towers.
The cavern is covered in stalactites/stalagmites formed by the waterfalls and river that flow through the city.
It is a dark land, with most of the light coming from several species of bioluminescent fungus and glowworms.

Murkwell

Grude’s home, Murkwell, is lush forest divided by a deadly acidic swamp.
The foggy swamp is surrounded by hazardous cliff and mountainous terrain.
Cross any of the destructible bridges at your own risk and beware of the spiky vegetation.
